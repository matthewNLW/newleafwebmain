Our Process.

01 — Discovery

Discovery

We learn about your business, goals, audience, and current digital presence through an initial form fill out, a short discovery call, and intake review.

Your Role

  • Complete initial discovery form
  • Share business context and goals
  • Explain what success looks like

Our Role

  • Ask the right questions
  • Identify opportunities and constraints
  • Define project scope and direction
Outcome

A shared understanding of goals, priorities, and scope.

02 — Onboarding

Onboarding

We formalize the agreement with a detailed quote and 50% deposit, then set up our communication channels to hit the ground running.

Your Role

  • Review quote and sign contract
  • Submit 50% deposit
  • Join project Slack channel
  • Provide necessary access

Our Role

  • Formalize scope & send invoice
  • Set up project repository & dashboard
  • Schedule kickoff meeting
Outcome

Administrative setup complete; ready for strategy.

03 — Strategy

Strategy

We define the structure of your website and how each page supports your business objectives.

Your Role

  • Review and approve the proposed structure
  • Confirm priorities and messaging direction

Our Role

  • Create sitemap and content flow
  • Align structure with conversion goals
  • Clarify technical requirements
Outcome

An approved sitemap and content plan.

04 — Design

Design

We establish the visual direction and design the homepage as the foundation for the entire site.

Your Role

  • Provide brand assets (if available)
  • Give clear, consolidated feedback

Our Role

  • Define typography, color, and layout system
  • Design a cohesive, professional interface
  • Apply revisions within agreed scope
Outcome

An approved design system and homepage direction.

05 — Build

Build

We develop the site using clean, responsive code and implement all approved pages and features.

Your Role

  • Minimal involvement unless questions arise
  • Timely responses if clarification is needed

Our Role

  • Build all pages and components
  • Ensure performance, responsiveness, and usability
  • Implement forms and integrations
Outcome

A fully built, functional website ready for review.

06 — Review & Launch

Review & Launch

You review the site, we refine details, and prepare everything for launch.

Your Role

  • Review the site thoroughly
  • Submit feedback in a single, organized round
  • Submit final 50% payment

Our Role

  • Apply final refinements
  • Handle technical launch details
  • Ensure everything works as expected
Outcome

Your website is live and ready for users.

07 — Ongoing Support

Ongoing Support

After launch, we remain available for support and offer ongoing maintenance options.

Your Role

  • Decide on ongoing support or maintenance
  • Share feedback as your business evolves

Our Role

  • Provide post-launch support
  • Keep your site updated, secure, and performing well
Outcome

Long-term stability and continued improvement.

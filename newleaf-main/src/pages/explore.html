  • Fantasy

    Immersive Medieval

    Evoke the grandeur of ancient legends with parchment textures, classic serif typography, and subtle ember effects. Perfect for storytelling brands that claim a rich heritage.

    Key Features Quest Log cursor, Parchment textures
    Palette Dark Brown, Parchment White, Gold
    Visuals Ember particles
    View Design Intent

    Feeling: A sense of ancient legacy and discovery.

    Why: To immerse users in a narrative-heavy experience that feels like uncovering a rare artifact.

  • Retro

    Nostalgic Arcade

    Channel the raw energy of the 80s and 90s arcade scene with monospaced fonts, vibrant neon accents, and CRT scanline overlays. A bold choice for brands refusing to blend in.

    Key Features CRT scanlines, Arcade buttons
    Palette Near Black, Retro Green
    Typography IBM Plex Mono
    View Design Intent

    Feeling: Energetic, raw, and nostalgic.

    Why: To disrupt standard corporate sterility with a fun, memorable personality that demands attention.

    Aa

  • Future

    Space Sci-Fi

    Command authority with deep void backgrounds, holographic HUD elements, and precision animation. Ideal for AI, tech, and security sectors requiring a cutting-edge aesthetic.

    Key Features Starfield bg, HUD panels, Glow effects
    Palette Deep Void, Starlight White, Cyan
    Visuals Animated spaceships
    View Design Intent

    Feeling: Precision, velocity, and limitless scale.

    Why: To project authority and cutting-edge capability for high-tech or AI-driven solutions.

    Aa

  • Nature

    Organic Editorial

    Establish immediate trust through organic color palettes, breathable whitespace, and pixel-art botanical accents. A serene backdrop for wellness, environmental, or lifestyle brands.

    Key Features Forest intro, Canopy hero
    Palette Deep Forest, Light Moss, Sage
    Aesthetics Pixel moss
    View Design Intent

    Feeling: Grounded, breathable, and calm.

Our Story.

My journey as a developer and designer began in the high-energy world of startups at NADA in Columbia, SC. There, I started by building in Bubble to deliver a comprehensive volunteer management platform. It was a crash course in technical scalability, but it also opened my eyes to a much larger reality.

While working there, I saw that outside the startup bubble, too many passionate small business owners were struggling. They had incredible products and services but lacked the digital infrastructure to showcase them effectively. I realized that these businesses deserved the same level of technical excellence and strategic design reserved for tech giants.

I founded New Leaf Web Development to bridge that gap. This agency was born from a desire to help local businesses turn the page, to move beyond basic websites and embrace digital tools that actively boost their business to the next level.
